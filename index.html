Jessica Lieberman

Learning Specialist

About

  • Jessica has a Master’s Degree in special education and was a classroom teacher and administrator in public and private schools for 15 years. For the last five years she has been a Learning Specialist in private practice working with students with learning disabilities, ADHD, and emotional challenges, particularly anxiety.
  • Her students range from elementary through high school, as well as post-secondary. Given her work with students on the northshore and in the city, she is familiar with numerous districts, schools/personnel, and curriculums.

E-Learning

  • Jessica is able to work with students virtually to support executive functioning skills (organizing assignments, scheduling, executing), teach and reinforce academic content, address IEP goals, and provide emotional support.
  • During these uncertain times, reduced fees/sliding scales are being offered for families facing financial distress/loss.
